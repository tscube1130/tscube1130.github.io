Hi! I am Sreeja, a Computer Science Undergraduate at Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IIT Guwahati), India.

I am drawn to systems programming — building things that are fast, correct, and close to the hardware. My projects span low-latency trading engines, compilers, virtual machines, and custom processors on FPGAs.

## Technical Interests

- **Systems & Low-Latency Programming:** I enjoy squeezing performance out of machines — zero-allocation pipelines, cache-aligned memory, branchless parsing. Building NanoMatch taught me that nanoseconds are earned, not assumed.

- **Compilers & Language Runtimes:** Designing a language, building its parser, generating bytecode, and running it on a VM I wrote from scratch — this is the kind of work that makes everything else click.

- **Computer Architecture:** Architecting a 3-stage pipelined processor in Verilog and watching it run a real game on an FPGA is one of the most satisfying things I have done.

## Non-Technical Interests

I enjoy mentoring — I was selected as one of 11 mentors for a JEE prep cohort of 400+ students and was awarded Best Mentor. Outside of tech, I like exploring music and staying up too late reading about things I have no business reading about.
