## Overview

A hardware-accelerated Chrome Dino clone running on an FPGA, powered by a custom
3-stage pipelined processor designed from scratch in Verilog.

## What Makes This Interesting

- Custom 3-stage pipeline as the core execution engine
- **Multiplayer** via Pmod GPIO — real-time synchronization signals across two FPGA boards
- LFSR-based pseudorandom cactus spawning
- 7-segment display multiplexers for score display
- Built as a 5-person team (CS224 Course Project)
